Let's Be S.M.A.R.T. Kittypicker

Let's Be S.M.A.R.T. runs a cat sanctuary located near Athens, Greece. All cats at the rescue shelter are fully vaccinated and sterilized, and they have been medically cared for and brought to health. Our cats are seen regularly by our local vet that visits on site, and each cat has his/her own medical record book and is microchipped. The cats are clean and well socialized and are ready to integrate easily into a private home. There is no fee for adoptions, but donations are always welcome!
